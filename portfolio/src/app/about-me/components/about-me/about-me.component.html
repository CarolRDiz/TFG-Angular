Hello! I'm Laura Rodiz

Sarah Webb is a freelance illustrator, visual development and background artist, and cartoonist from Alaska. She currently lives in Los Angeles, California, and graduated from MICA in Baltimore, Maryland.

Her work focuses on creating immersive worlds and narratives, and is inspired by the otherworldly and odd within everyday life and nature. She is always interested in working on sapphic, fantasy/slice-of-life stories in any medium.
